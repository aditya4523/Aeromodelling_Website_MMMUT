Our Story

As the official aeromodelling division of the SAE Collegiate Club at MMMUT, Gorakhpur, we are a community driven by a shared passion for aerospace innovation and hands-on engineering. Our primary mission is to provide students a platform for practical experience in aircraft design, fabrication, and flight testing. By participating in different challenging competitions, we push ourselves to devise creative solutions for complex aerospace challenges. Our team unites students from diverse engineering backgrounds to collaboratively build aircraft tailored to specific mission goals.

Design & Structures

Crafting cutting-edge UAV designs using advanced CAD modeling, structural analysis, and aerodynamic simulations.

Our Workspace

Our dedicated workshop is where ideas take flight. Equipped for design, fabrication, and testing, this is the heart of our innovation. Here, we utilize cutting-edge tools for 3D printing, laser cutting, and composite manufacturing to bring our ambitious projects from digital blueprints to flight-ready prototypes.

Our Mission

To innovate and educate through cutting-edge UAV technology and hands-on learning, empowering students to solve real-world aerospace challenges.

Our Vision

To break down the financial barriers to aerospace innovation. We aim to create an inclusive environment where any passionate student, regardless of their economic background, has the opportunity to learn, build, and master UAV technology.
