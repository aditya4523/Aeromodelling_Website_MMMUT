Competitions

We regularly challenge ourselves in national-level competitions, pushing the boundaries of aerodynamic design and engineering excellence.

Aerothon

AEROTHON, organized by SAE India, is a national-level competition that challenges students to design and innovate Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) for solving real-world problems.

Read More

National Boeing

The main fixed-wing competition sponsored by Boeing is the Boeing National Aeromodelling Competition. Participants in this competition design, build, and fly fixed-wing aircraft models under specific performance criteria.

SIH

Smart India Hackathon is a nationwide initiative providing students a platform to solve pressing problems through product innovation and creative technology.

Team Achievements

Aerothon 2022 – National Champion

Our journey began with Aerothon 2022, where Team Starfleet proudly secured First Place. Competing against teams from across the country, we not only won the final round but also bagged the First Prize at the national level in the very first round.

Aerothon 2023 – Consecutive Qualification

Building upon our previous success, Team Starfleet once again made its mark by qualifying in both the First and Second Rounds, ensuring a strong and consistent presence among India’s top drone innovators.

Smart India Hackathon (Internal Round) 2025 – Winner

Continuing our streak, we clinched the First Prize in the Internal Round of the Smart India Hackathon (SIH) 2025 with a project that demonstrated creativity and technical proficiency.
